FAQs about Dominical, Costa Rica

Q: What is the best time to visit Dominical?

A: The best time to visit Dominical depends on your preferences. The dry season, from December to April, offers sunny skies and calm waters, ideal for swimming and sunbathing. The wet season, from May to November, brings abundant rainfall, lush greenery, and powerful waves, perfect for surfing.

Q: What are the main activities in Dominical?

A: Dominical offers a diverse range of activities, including surfing, swimming, snorkeling, hiking, zip-lining, horseback riding, and wildlife viewing.

Q: What is the transportation like in Dominical?

A: Dominical is a small town, and most places can be reached on foot or by bicycle. Taxis and buses are available for longer distances.

Q: What is the cost of living in Dominical?
